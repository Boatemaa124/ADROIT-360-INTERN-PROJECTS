Overview

*Rush Hour* (1998) is an action-comedy film directed by Brett Ratner, starring Jackie Chan and Chris Tucker. The story follows Inspector Lee (Jackie Chan), a skilled and disciplined Hong Kong cop, and Detective James Carter (Chris Tucker), a loud-mouthed LAPD officer, who are forced to work together to rescue the kidnapped daughter of a Chinese diplomat. Despite their contrasting personalities and cultural differences, the two form an unlikely but effective partnership as they navigate a series of dangerous and comedic situations while solving the crime. The film is known for its blend of martial arts, humor, and action-packed sequences, making it a popular success and leading to two sequels.
